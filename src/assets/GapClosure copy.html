Dental Care

Overview

The Dental Care Department at Saroj Super Speciality Hospital is dedicated to providing exceptional oral health services through a patient-centric approach. We are equipped with advanced laser technology, allowing us to offer a diverse range of treatments tailored to individual needs. Our team of skilled dental surgeons provides a comprehensive array of services, from routine check-ups to complex surgical procedures, all customized to suit the unique requirements of each patient.

We prioritize the well-being and satisfaction of our patients, ensuring they receive high-quality care in a comfortable setting. Whether it's cosmetic enhancements for a confident smile or surgical interventions for complex dental issues, our goal remains steadfast—to deliver compassionate and effective dental care that promotes long-term oral health and enhances overall quality of life.

Common Diseases
There are numerous dental conditions that individuals may encounter, including but not limited to:
  • Tooth decay (cavities)
  • Impacted wisdom teeth
  • Gum disease (periodontitis)
  • Tooth sensitivity
  • Bad breath (halitosis)
  • Toothache
  • Oral thrush (candidiasis)
  • Dry mouth (xerostomia)
  • Mouth sores (canker sores)
  • Tooth erosion
